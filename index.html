Basic Hand Signs

Rat (Ne, 子)

Left thumb on the outside.

Ox (Ushi, 丑)

Right hand horizontal, left hand vertical.

Tiger (Tora, 寅)

Both thumbs straight up.

Hare (U, 卯)

Aside from pinky, right fingers gently curled.

Dragon (Tatsu, 辰)

Left thumb on top.

Snake (Mi, 巳)

Left thumb on the outside.

Horse (Uma, 午)

Both elbows out, index fingers form a triangle.

Ram (Hitsuji, 未)

Left thumb on top, hands together vertically.

Monkey (Saru, 申)

Thumbs lie flat on pinkies.

Bird (Tori, 酉)

Be careful with the angle of the thumbs and the location of the fingertips.

Dog (Inu, 戌)

Left hand flat on right fist.

Boar (I, 亥)

Wrists need great flexibility.
